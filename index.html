Who We Are
It is a new and exciting breed of full service VoIP carrier operating our own robust network. Exclusively serving Australian businesses, the company was formed in 2011 to fill a gap in the market by providing affordable, high grade, private network VoIP trunks.

With over seven years experience building and delivering IP PBX's and IP telephony systems for a wide range of businesses, organisations and service providers throughout Australia and New Zealand, our team operates at the forefront of telephony at what is a turning point for the industry.
Combing the power and possibilities of new and emerging communication technologies with excellent support and service, we can offer you an alternative to the restrictive and costly traditional telephony options offered by the big telcos.

Specifically designed to meet the needs and ambitions of growing businesses in the mid market, our approach is refreshingly different from what you’ve come to expect.

We love technology and innovation but we won’t confuse you with geek speak. We’re all about being open, honest and keeping things simple – no plans, no hidden costs, no tricks – just a fresh outlook from an approachable company, determined to offer our clients a brighter kind of telephony.
